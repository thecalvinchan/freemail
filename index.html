Email has been around for over 40 years. Within these past 40 years, there have been countless attempts at replacing email. But that's not something that can be done overnight. If we want to fully replace email with another social medium, we have to ease ourself into communicating without it. We have to phase out email, one step at a time.

Introducing Freemail, a service that helps you ease out of using email. We forward any emails that you receive to your social network of choice. There, you can reply directly to the message, and we will make sure that it gets delivered back to the correct recipient.
